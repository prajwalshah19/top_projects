About Me

Welcome to The Arbor Montessori School! My name is Rakhi Shah. I live in Lisle, IL with my husband and two kids. I have strong passion of teaching and found out that Montessori methods are most conducive for young minds.In the past I have worked at Montessori schools in Lisle and Aurora, IL handling classrooms as lead teacher. I have enjoyed working with teachers trained through American Montessori Society (AMS). I began my own fully accredited Montessori teaching practice by setting up a Montessori-oriented classroom in my house and would love to use montessori principles to teach your kids which will better prepare their minds in right directions of thinking and doing.
